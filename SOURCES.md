# Sources & References

## Primary Sources

### Old Norse Texts

- **Prose Edda** (c. 1220). Snorri Sturluson. Primary source for Norse mythology including Valkyrie mythology.
- **Poetic Edda** (c. 9th-13th century). Collection of Old Norse poems. Contains earliest Valkyrie references.
- **Völsunga saga** (c. 13th century). Contains the story of Brynhildr, the most famous Valkyrie.
- **Helgakviða Hundingsbana** (Poetic Edda). Features the Valkyrie Sigrún.
- **Darraðarljóð** (11th century). Poem depicting Valkyries weaving the fates of warriors.

### Historical Sources

- Sturluson, S. (c. 1220). *Heimskringla*. Contains references to Valkyries in Norwegian king sagas.
- Various Icelandic sagas mentioning Valkyries in battle contexts.
